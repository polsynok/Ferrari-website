Racing Roots:

Enzo Ferrari, the founder, was a racing driver himself, and his company's origins are deeply intertwined with racing, particularly Formula 1.

The Prancing Horse:

The prancing horse logo, a symbol of Ferrari, was originally the emblem of a World War I Italian fighter pilot, Francesco Baracca, and his mother gifted it to Enzo Ferrari after his death.

Formula 1 Dominance:

Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in Formula 1 history, with a record number of constructors' championships and drivers' world championships.

Red Racing Tradition:

The iconic red color of Ferrari cars stems from a tradition in the early days of auto racing, where Italian Grand Prix race cars were required to be red.

Discover the World of Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the most renowned and luxurious sports car brands in the world, offering incredible performance, cutting-edge technology, and timeless designs. From the Ferrari 488 to the Ferrari SF90 Stradale, each model represents the perfect balance of speed, luxury, and engineering excellence.
