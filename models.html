Racing Roots:

Enzo Ferrari, the founder, was a racing driver himself, and his company's origins are deeply intertwined with racing, particularly Formula 1.

The Prancing Horse:

The prancing horse logo, a symbol of Ferrari, was originally the emblem of a World War I Italian fighter pilot, Francesco Baracca, and his mother gifted it to Enzo Ferrari after his death.

Formula 1 Dominance:

Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in Formula 1 history, with a record number of constructors' championships and drivers' world championships.

Red Racing Tradition:

The iconic red color of Ferrari cars stems from a tradition in the early days of auto racing, where Italian Grand Prix race cars were required to be red.

Popular Ferrari Models

Ferrari 488 GTB

The Ferrari 488 GTB is a perfect blend of power, luxury, and cutting-edge technology. Powered by a 3.9L V8 engine, it can reach 0-60 mph in just 3 seconds.

Ferrari F8 Tributo

The Ferrari F8 Tributo features the same 3.9L turbocharged V8 as the 488 but with even more refined performance and agility. It delivers incredible handling and a top speed of 211 mph.

Ferrari SF90 Stradale

With its hybrid powertrain and 986 horsepower, the Ferrari SF90 Stradale is Ferrari's most powerful road car ever. It combines traditional V8 power with electric motors for a thrilling experience.
